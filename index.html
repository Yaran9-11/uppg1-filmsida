The Amazing Spider-Man

In the The Amazing Spider-Man we get to follow the life of Peter Parker. He is the average teenage boy who has a crush on a girl. One day he gets bitten by a genetically modified spider and gains superpowers. He decides to use his powers to fight crime and protect the city from villians. The villian in this movie is the lizard, he was a mad scientiest who wanted to cure his son from a disease.

He finally beats the lizard and then we get to see him grieve and try to distance himself from his crush. The movie ends with Peter Parker hinting at trying to get with his crush.
